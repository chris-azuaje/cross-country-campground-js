A full-service campground located close to Lake Norman, Concord Mills Mall and Lowes Motor Speedway

Over 400 campsites nestled in refreshing wooded areas, most with full hookup and 30-50 amp service. Complete heated bath-house, laundry and onsite store. Activities abound with Carpet Golf, Shuffle Board, Indoor Games, Tennis, Fishing, Swimming, Basketball, Nature Hiking and even much more!
